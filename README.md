Jumia Shop Performance Dashboard
Project Overview

**This project focuses on analyzing product performance on Jumia using Excel. The dashboard helps explore how pricing, discounts, reviews, and ratings impact product performance and customer engagement.**

Dataset

The dataset includes:

Product – Product name

Current Price – Selling price (KSh)

Old Price – Original price (KSh)

Discount – Discount percentage

Reviews – Number of customer reviews

Rating – Average rating out of 5

Data Preparation

Handle missing values and duplicates

Convert prices and discounts to numeric format

Convert ratings to numeric values

Create additional columns:

Discount Amount (KSh)

Rating Category: Poor, Average, Excellent

Discount Category: Low, Medium, High

Analysis

Descriptive statistics: average prices, discounts, ratings; most and least expensive products

Trend analysis: relationships between discounts, ratings, and number of reviews

Product performance: top products by discount, reviews, and rating

Dashboard Features

Overview: total products, average rating, discount, reviews

Product Performance: top products by rating, reviews, and discount

Trend Analysis: charts showing discount vs reviews, rating vs reviews

Product Categories: breakdown by rating and discount categories

Interactive with Pivot Tables, charts, conditional formatting, and slicers
